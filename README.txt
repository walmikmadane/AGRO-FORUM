AGRO-FORUM
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.Visit the web application hosted at the following link:-
	https://agro-forum.shinyapps.io/agro-forum/
2.Put in the corresponding inputs viz:-
	State,Area Under Irrigation,Total available area,Select Rainfall,Select Theme for GUI(optional)
3.The output will be shown in the following terms:-
	a.Rice Yield Prediction
	b.State Market Prices
	c.All India Market Prices
	d.Current Trending News
	e.Traders Reported
